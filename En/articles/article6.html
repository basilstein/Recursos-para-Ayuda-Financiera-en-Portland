Federal Student Loans

Federal student loans usually have lower interest rates than private loans. When they’re based on financial need, they can be subsidized, meaning the government will pay for the interest of the loan. They can also be subsidized, but unsubsidized loans still give you more options on how to pay them off.

You can find more information on the Federal Student Aid website, or from this webpage from Portland Public Schools which has information about many kinds of academic financial aid.
