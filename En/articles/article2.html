PCC Scholarship

What is the purpose of this site

  • To guide PCC students to complete scholarships relevant to the college, unfortunately it is long and overcomplicates things.

Essentially, there are the PCC Foundation scholarships, as well as external scholarships which PCC reccomends you apply to.

  • To apply to the PCC Foundation scholarships, you just need to complete the financial aid application and create a MyPCC account, and complete desired scholarships before March 1st.
  • With regard to external scholarships, PCC encourages students to apply to OSAC scholarships, as well as other common Scholarship websites such as Scholarship America.
  • PCC emphasizes that external scholarship applications should be completed in the Winter to best accomadate the PCC schedule.
